About

Security advice that stays useful under stress

Plaintext Security is a practical education project focused on scam prevention and secure builder habits.

Why this exists

Most security guidance is either too technical or too vague. We publish plain-English checklists, realistic examples, and response playbooks that people can actually use in time-sensitive moments.

Our principles: calm tone, no shame, and no theatrics. Security education should reduce panic, not create it.

Who this is for

  • Everyday people trying to avoid common digital traps
  • Founders and developers shipping quickly with limited security support
  • AI builders who need practical controls for agent and tool risk
