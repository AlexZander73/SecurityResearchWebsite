Localized QR and payment scam patterns

Same scam structure, different local wrapping. Use these examples to spot familiar tricks faster.

Parking zones

Fake stickers added over meter instructions in busy street parking areas.

  • Code points to unknown domain
  • No app receipt after payment

Transit top-ups

QR “top-up now” posters near stations during peak times.

  • Urgent countdown timer
  • Asks extra identity data

Event payments

Temporary signs for fast lane/entry upgrades at venues.

  • One-time payment via unknown gateway
  • No official confirmation

Community examples by context

Patterns currently tracked: city parking, weekend markets, festival entry, delivery locker notices, and school fundraiser payment links.
