Flagship Interactive Track

Dating-sim style scenario trainer

This track turns familiar dating-app and texting situations into short branching lessons. The point is to build instinct around pressure tactics before money or credentials are exposed.

Relatable risk moments

Fast emotional bonding, urgent money requests, off-platform moves, “prove trust” links, fake emergencies, and crypto pressure pitches.

How learners interact

Choose replies in chat-style branches, get immediate risk signal feedback, then receive a calm debrief with next-step habits.

Design principle

No shame, no fear language, no gotcha framing. The experience should feel realistic, practical, and useful under stress.
